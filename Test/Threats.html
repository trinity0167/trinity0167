Malicous Threats

- In the online world, there are many threats that put you in danger. Phishing is one threat, it is done by scammers who pretend to be a known orginisation and will attempt to get you to click fake links or send them valuable information.
- Viruses are another threat that are typcially downloaded onto your hardware by accident, they will then start executing code to damage and steal your data.
- Hackers are another threat too, they are highly trained people that know how to break into your system and steal what they want.
